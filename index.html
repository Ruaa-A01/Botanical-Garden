ABOUT US

Welcome to our Botanical Garden — a warm, living space where plants, people, and community meet. We care for diverse collections, run hands-on learning programs, and welcome visitors of all ages.

♥︎


Our Mission

We are dedicated to preserving plant biodiversity while educating and empowering our community to cultivate sustainable gardens and protect our environment for generations to come.


Through thoughtful stewardship, seasonal beauty, and shared knowledge, we create a welcoming sanctuary where nature, heritage, and community flourish together.

Our Story

Established in 1998 by a small circle of local botanists and volunteers, our garden has since flourished into a 12-acre thoughtfully curated haven of themed plant collections, living research grounds, and community programs rooted in shared learning and seasonal beauty.


“Where wildflowers meet wisdom, and every unfolding petal whispers a promise of tomorrow.”

The Team

Our skilled horticulturists, knowledgeable educators, and committed volunteers meticulously maintain our curated collections while offering thoughtfully guided tours, refined workshops, and engaging school programs.


We are passionate about sharing our love of plants and fostering a deeper connection between people and nature through our work.

Get Involved

Join our growing circle — become a member, lend your hands on community workdays, or gather with us for seasonal celebrations that sustain and protect the beauty of our living landscape.

Our story unfolds in petals and pathways, in the rhythm of seasons and the laughter of visitors — and your presence can add a new flourish to every chapter.


“Together, we cultivate a greener tomorrow — one seed, one smile, one shared moment at a time.”

OUR PLANTS

We have a diverse collection of plants from around the world, including rare and endangered species. Our garden features themed sections such as tropical, desert, and medicinal plants.


Explore our diverse plant collection and discover the beauty of nature.

Amaryllis Fomossissima

Amaryllis formosissima rises with dramatic elegance, its tall, sturdy stalks crowned by trumpet-shaped blooms of deep crimson and coral.

Each blossom bursts like a radiant jewel, commanding attention while infusing the garden with passion and vitality.

Canna Glauca

A striking tropical beauty, Canna Glauca features broad, lush green leaves and clusters of vibrant blooms that rise like torch flames.

Perfect for creating bold, exotic focal points in garden borders or containers.

Curcuma Longa (Turmeric)

Curcuma Longa's delicate lavender-pink bracts crown golden roots hidden beneath the soil.

A plant of both beauty and bounty, it thrives in shaded warmth, whispering stories of spice, tradition, and exotic allure.

French Lavender (Lavandula Dentata)

Soft silvery foliage meets fragrant purple blossoms in French Lavender, filling the air with timeless calm.

A companion to butterflies and bees alike, it evokes the sun-drenched charm of Provence in every breeze.

Iris Germanica (Bearded Iris)

Iris Germanica, or bearded iris, showcases bold, sword-like leaves and dramatic, intricately ruffled flowers in a spectrum of colors.

Perfect for creating vertical interest and a touch of old-world elegance.

Iris Plicata (Painted Iris)

With its strikingly patterned petals, Iris Plicata brings a painterly touch to your garden.

Each bloom features delicate stripes and veining that make this iris a conversation starter in mixed borders or containers.

Lachenalia Tricolor

Delicate and enchanting, Lachenalia tricolor offers slender, pendulous flowers in soft gradients of cream, green, and rose-pink.

Ideal for rock gardens, intimate beds, or whimsical garden corners, this South African beauty adds subtle poetry and sculptural charm to any landscape.

Ornithogalum Aureum (Golden Star)

Clusters of golden stars rise from tall, graceful stems, their radiant petals capturing sunlight like tiny lanterns.

Ornithogalum Aureum brings warmth, cheer, and resilient charm to sunny garden retreats.

Polygonatum Multiflora (Solomon's Seal)

Polygonatum Multiflora dances on arching stems, with bell-shaped flowers that dangle delicately like lanterns of light.

In shaded garden corners, it whispers quiet elegance, inviting reflection and wonder.

Polygonatum Verticillatum (Whorled Solomon's Seal)

Polished and poised, Polygonatum Verticillatum features whorled leaves and creamy-white blooms that float gracefully above soft greenery.

Its understated architecture adds serenity and sculptural beauty to woodland-inspired spaces.

Tigridia Pavonia (Peacock Flower)

Tigridia Pavonia's tiger-striped petals burst like fleeting sunsets, each bloom a spectacular one-day wonder.

Exotic and bold, it transforms borders and containers into ephemeral theaters of color and drama.

Tulipa Oculus Colis (Eye Tulip)

Tulipa 'Oculus Colis' dazzles with radiant petals and a mesmerising “eye” at its heart.

Its springtime blossoms create moments of whimsy and elegance, turning any garden bed into a tapestry of color and charm.
